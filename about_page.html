About ZooCalendar

Welcome to ZooCalendar.com – your comprehensive guide to discovering amazing zoo experiences across the United States!

Our Mission

ZooCalendar is dedicated to connecting animal enthusiasts, families, and wildlife lovers with zoos and wildlife parks throughout America. We believe that zoos play a vital role in conservation, education, and inspiring the next generation of wildlife advocates.

Our goal is simple: Make it easy for everyone to discover, explore, and visit incredible zoos in their state and beyond. Whether you're planning a family vacation, looking for a weekend adventure, or searching for educational experiences, ZooCalendar is your trusted companion.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Zoo Directory: Browse zoos in all 50 states plus Washington D.C., with direct links to official websites
  • Easy State Search: Quickly find zoos in any state with our intuitive search feature and autocomplete functionality
  • Detailed Information: Learn about each zoo's location, size, and notable features
  • User Community: Share your experiences and read feedback from fellow zoo enthusiasts
  • Personalized Experience: Create an account to save your favorite zoos and get personalized recommendations

Why Visit Zoos?

Modern zoos are centers of conservation, research, and education. They provide:

  • Safe havens for endangered species and breeding programs
  • Educational opportunities to learn about wildlife and ecosystems
  • Research facilities advancing our understanding of animal behavior and conservation
  • Community gathering spaces that inspire environmental stewardship
  • Memorable family experiences and connections with nature

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date information about zoos across the country. We continuously update our database to ensure you have access to the latest information about facilities, exhibits, and special events.

ZooCalendar supports responsible wildlife tourism and encourages visitors to:

  • Support AZA-accredited institutions (Association of Zoos and Aquariums)
  • Participate in conservation education programs
  • Respect wildlife and follow zoo guidelines
  • Support conservation efforts through zoo membership and donations

Looking Ahead

We're continuously working to enhance your experience with features like:

  • Event calendars for special zoo programs and exhibits
  • User reviews and ratings
  • Interactive maps and directions
  • Mobile app for on-the-go zoo discovery
  • Educational resources and wildlife facts

Get in Touch

Have questions, suggestions, or want to report missing information? We'd love to hear from you! Use our comments feature on the main page to share your feedback.

Together, we can help people discover the wonder of wildlife and support conservation efforts nationwide.

Thank you for being part of the ZooCalendar community!
